Front-End Developer

Delloite Brussels Full Time
Save Job
Apply Now

Job Description

As a Front-End Developer in our Digital Experience team, you are responsible for developing cutting-edge digital solutions. You will work alongside some of the best developers, designers and strategy minds in the business, delivering top-notch digital solutions for the biggest and best players on the market.

Our developers aren't people who are locked away in a room churning out code to match a large set of specifications that were defined in another time and place. Instead, they work side-by-side with our clients and the rest of the team, combining deep technical expertise with innovative thinking and a strong understanding of what makes businesses and their customers tick. It's the combination of these skills and way of working that helps us deliver the best digital experiences.

Are you motivated to combine your sense for digital with the art of programming? Do you look beyond the code and back-end solution? Are you interested in how mobile applications tap into this? Are you curious to see how front-end solutions can empower your work rather than limit it? In short, do you see the big picture and the big role your back-end expertise will play in it?

Then you are Deloitte Digital's next Front-End Developer.

Responsibilities

  • We believe in Agile and you will be part of a Scrum team in which you are collaborate with the rest of the team to achieve the best deliverable possible for each sprint. The team is often a mix of client and Deloitte's personnel working together.
  • During the sprint planning, you will decide how to best implement the technical stories and participate in the story estimations. Together with your peers you will align on the approach and how this will fit within the overall architecture of the project
  • Within each sprint, you take ownership of the stories you work on and analyze, develop, test and release them. You align closely with the other developers in order to ensure that the right person works on the right task and you even benefit from peer review throughout the sprint. We work with tools that help you move ahead faster, such as JIRA, Confluence, Jenkins, Bitbucket, etc.
  • On top of pure frontend skills, you have insight into the backend and design work, so that you are efficient in the collaboration within the team.
  • On top of regular sprint work, you provide our clients with advice on digital change, helping them to exploit web, mobile and social media to strengthen their brand and grow their business.

Your Profile

  • Bachelor or Master degree combined with post-graduate experience, preferably including client-facing consulting role(s);
  • Experience producing web / mobile / enterprise solutions in a professional development environment;
  • A passion for digital transformation and technology;
  • HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript are your bread and butter. You can tell us all about the strengths and weaknesses of React, Vue, Angular, Bootstrap, Knockout, and Handlebars. In short, front-end is your expertise;
  • Ideally you have worked on project in which you integrated this front-end savviness with management systems (e.g. Salesforce, Adobe Experience, SAP Hybris);
  • You are a generalist in your field of expertise with hybrid interests in all technology beyond that, such as back-end development (e.g. Java, NodeJS, .NET) and/or design (e.g. UX expertise, Photoshop, Sketch);

Our Offer

  • A challenging, innovating environment where personal development and growth are limitless;
  • An attractive and competitive salary with fringe benefits;
  • Working within an entrepreneurial team of digital pioneers with ambitious growth plans in the coming years;
  • Our business activities are centralized in Deloitte's Gateway office based in Brussels. Projects could be both nationally (BE) or internationally (EU) based.
